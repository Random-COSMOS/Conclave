Digital Art

Digital Art is an artistic composition that relies heavily on the use of technology in its creation. It's been around since the 70's and is used mainly for advertising and visual for film. Digital Art comes in many forms, most often it's either solely computer generated, drawn various types of software, or digital paintings sourced from photos. Digital art couldn't really exist without computers. Those machines so us today got their start in the 1940s, when the first true computer, the Electronic Integrator and Computer, or the ENIAC, was created for military purposes. Artists first began exploring possibilities of art from computers and related technologies in the late 1950s and early 1960s.
